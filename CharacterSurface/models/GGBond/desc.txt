Meet our cheerful little mascot, a round bundle of curiosity who greets every visitor with the same wide, fearless smile. He was dreamed up to be the friendly face of small adventures, the companion who tugs your sleeve toward the next discovery and never lets a rainy afternoon feel dull. His colours are bright on purpose, chosen to lift the corner of a tired mouth into a grin. Wherever he goes he leaves a trail of small kindnesses and louder laughter, reminding everyone he meets that the best journeys are the ones we take together.
